# Detail gradient (3-30-300)

Where things go on the page. Most important + least detail at top; most detail + least important at bottom.

## Pattern

```
+----------------------------------------------+
|  Zone 1: Summary (KPIs, Cards)               |  y: 24 – ~200
|  Most important, least detail                |
+----------------------------------------------+
|  Zone 2: Analysis (Charts, Trends)           |  y: ~216 – ~600
|  Context, patterns, comparisons              |
+----------------------------------------------+
|  Zone 3: Detail (Tables, Matrices)           |  y: ~616 – bottom
|  Precise values, drill-down                  |
+----------------------------------------------+
```

## Heights by zone

- Zone 1 (summary): 130–200 px
- Zone 2 (analysis): 350–450 px
- Zone 3 (detail): 350–450 px

## Visual count per zone

- Zone 1: 3–5 cards/KPIs in a row
- Zone 2: 1–3 charts
- Zone 3: 1 table or matrix, usually full-width

## Why

Readers scan top-left → bottom-right. The first thing they see should be the headline metric, not a raw table.
